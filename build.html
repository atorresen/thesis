This is a project that builds art with math. This is a project that builds math with art. This is a project that wonders if art and math are made of the same stuff, and what that stuff is, and how we might invite others to grow to love it.

In bouncing between art and math, and art with math, and math with art, we arrive not at an average, but in a new place. We belong here, and our confusion and our feelings about being here belong here too. We build a room where everyone is invited.
