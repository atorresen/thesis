HELP ME MAKE ART WITH MATH

a Kolakoski sequence wire-folding activity

Hi! I’m AnneMarie, a grad student at RISD. I’m working on a project about experiencing math via art, and I want your help.


On Monday, March 2 at 5pm in Kassar Room 105, I’ll be holding a math/art workshop. I’ll introduce you to the Kolakoski sequence, then have you bend wire in a Kolakoski-sequence-inspired pattern. I’ll take your bent wires — and trade you a zine for your efforts :) — and assemble them into a sculpture that will appear at the RISD Grad Thesis Show (May 20-30 at the RI Convention Center). If you bend a wire, then your name will be listed as a contributing artist!

Please join us: 5pm, Monday March 2, in Kassar 105 — the event will last 30-40 minutes, and you don’t need to bring anything except your brain and your hands.
